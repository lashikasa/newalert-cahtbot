# AI News Alert Chatbot

The **AI News Alert Chatbot** is an interactive web application that combines **real-time news aggregation** with **AI-powered insights**. It allows users to browse trending news, interact with an AI assistant to get summaries or explanations, and engage with content using reactions.

---

## Features

- **News Dashboard**
  - Aggregates news from multiple categories: Trending, Live, Sports, Money, Weather, and Watch.
  - Displays news in a responsive **grid layout**.
  - Hover over news cards to **zoom** and play videos.
  - Click **Read More** to visit the original article.
  
- **Search and AI Interaction**
  - Search bar at the top for quick AI queries.
  - **Ask AI** button navigates to the AI chatbot directly.
  - Each news card has an **AI button** to analyze that article in the chatbot.

- **AI Chatbot**
  - Displays AI responses with:
    - Text summaries
    - Media attachments (images/videos)
    - Confidence scores (High / Medium / Low)
    - Sentiment analysis
    - Sources for verification
  - **Text-to-Speech** for AI replies.
  - Users can input their own queries for real-time answers.

- **Reactions**
  - Thumbs-up icon on news cards to like articles.
  - Clicking thumbs-up reveals other reactions (Haha, Wow, Sad, Angry) like on Facebook.
  - Double-click resets reactions to default.

## How to Use the Demo
1. **Browse News**: Scroll through the dashboard to view trending and live news.
2. **Interact with AI**:
   - Click **AI** on a news card to analyze it.
   - Use the search bar or **Ask AI** button to chat with the AI assistant.
3. **React to News**:
   - Click thumbs-up to like an article and show additional reactions.
   - Double-click thumbs-up to reset.
